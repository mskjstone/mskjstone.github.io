Man About Cake


Building brand awareness and delivering actionable inspiration to baking enthusiasts through a weekly YouTube series

In 2016 Bluprint tasked me with launching a new customer acquisition channel: YouTube. I worked with marketers and producers to research and analyze audience, content, and marketing strategies that would delight viewers and grow Bluprint's addressable audience.

Analyses and audience engagement strategies informed episodic content: here, wedding cakes, a YouTuber collaboration, and fan-inspired design

I collaborated with ecommerce partners to develop ancillary product

The brand became highly sought-after, yielding industry collaborations like NBC's Jurassic World

Episodes focused on "actionable inspiration" with relevant product



RESEARCH & ANALYSIS

During concept development, my research goals included understanding content strategies that would enable the series to perform well with YouTube's recommendation algorithm. As the show gained an audience, ongoing research & analysis between episode releases focused on audience engagement, growth, and revenue potential.

• Concept development: Performed domain reseach & competitive analysis on food edutainment channels
• Post-launch: Used YouTube & Google Analytics to optimize click-through and conversion rates
• Post-launch: Used YouTube timestamp analytics to determine most-watched segments
• Post-launch: Performed A/B testing to inform visual merchandising

DESIGN

I worked to ensure all content, scripting, and merchandising couched strategy within the show's entertainment and narrative.

• Performed SEO & trend analysis to inform new episode content
• Worked with ecommerce and production partners to naturally script popular product into episodes
• Tested a variety of site visit hooks to drive registrations and purchases

IMPACT

• In just three years the channel acquired over 800,000 subscribers, with some episodes accruing millions of views.
• The show pioneered production approaches for edutainment series on Bluprint's streaming subscription service.
