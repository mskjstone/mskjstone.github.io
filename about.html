Wonderful to have you! I'm Katie.

I'm a Master's student in Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University.

Over the past 8 years I have worked in the informal education sector across nonprofits and startups, from designing curricula for a research-based study abroad program in rural Thailand to wearing many hats at a Denver startup that makes online video classes for crafts and hobbies. I'm interested how systems, products and technology can create joyful, meaningful experiences while increasing human knowledge. I completed my undergrad at Northwestern University in 2011 and am a proud Wildcat!
