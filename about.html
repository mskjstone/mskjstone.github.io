Welcome! I'm Katie.


Over the past decade I have worked across nonprofits and startups, from designing curricula for a research-based study abroad program in rural Thailand to wearing many hats at an edtech startup acquired by NBCUniversal.

I love owning end-to-end product development, and I feel most fulfilled when I can flex my analytical and creative skillset in a collaborative, fast-paced environment.

My mantra: joyful, meaningful design.


In my personal and professional endeavors, this means creating products and experiences that are both enriching and necessary. I am passionate about:

• Bringing people together in meaningful ways through civic spaces and experiences;
• Transforming experiences of self-actualization, like personal finance, education, and home design; and
• Infusing my work with beauty, joy, and play.

Design brings joy & meaning to my life.
